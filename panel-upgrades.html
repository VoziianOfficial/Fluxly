When this path fits

Panel upgrades are often requested when the home needs more electrical capacity.

Homeowners may explore this category when adding major appliances, planning an EV charger, replacing an older panel, dealing with frequent breaker trips, or preparing for remodeling work. A provider may need to review the existing panel, available capacity, local code requirements, and any permit or inspection steps.

Fluxly does not inspect panels or perform electrical work. It helps homeowners organize the request so independent providers can discuss the project more clearly.

Provider evaluation

Compare more than the upgrade price.

Panel upgrade conversations can include capacity, utility coordination, permit requirements, inspection scheduling, grounding, labeling, materials, access, and warranty terms. Homeowners should compare the full scope and verify provider credentials before hiring.

Project details

Add practical panel details before requesting provider options.

You do not need to know every technical answer before submitting a request, but clear notes can help providers understand the situation faster.

⚡ Current panel notes

Share panel age, visible amperage if known, breaker issues, or photos of the panel area.

🔌 New electrical load

Mention EV chargers, appliances, HVAC changes, additions, remodels, or workshop equipment.

📍 Location and access

Add ZIP code, indoor or outdoor panel location, access limits, and preferred timing.

📋 Quote questions

Ask about permits, inspections, utility coordination, materials, warranty, and exclusions.

1. Overview

is an independent electrical provider matching platform. The website helps homeowners prepare project details and compare independent local electrical provider options. does not perform electrical work directly and does not operate as an electrical contractor.

2. Information we may collect

When you use this website or submit a request, we may collect information such as your name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, selected service type, project timing, project notes, and any other information you choose to provide.

We may also collect basic technical information such as browser type, device information, pages visited, referring pages, approximate location based on IP address, and interactions with forms or website features.

3. How information may be used

Information may be used to process matching requests, communicate with you, route requests by service category and location, improve the website, support security, prevent misuse, comply with legal obligations, and provide a better user experience.

4. Sharing with independent providers

If you submit a matching request, your information may be shared with independent local electrical service providers or related service partners so they can contact you about your project. Provider availability may vary by ZIP code, city, service type, timing, and other factors.

Independent providers are not employees of . Homeowners are responsible for verifying provider licenses, insurance, permits, qualifications, quote details, warranty terms, and all project requirements before hiring.
